Indo-France Seminar

2nd - 5th October 2023

We are pleased to announce Indo-France Seminar organized under CEFIPRA from October 2-5, 2023 at the Learn Education Institute (INST), Punjab, India. This seminar aims to strengthen the collaborative research network between France and India in the evolving field of metal nanoclusters and will help outline some perspectives for future collaborations on the diverse field of nanoclusters ranging from the design of self-assembled nanoclusters, and structure-property relationships to applications in energy conversion and nano-bio interface. The meeting will be held in Chandigarh, India, and promises to be an excellent platform for eminent scientists as well as young researchers from India and France to share their latest discoveries, which will aid in taking the collaborations to a larger scale. Chandigarh is one of India's most beautiful and well-planned cities, designed by the French architect Le Corbusier. The city is located near the foothills of the great Himalayas.
