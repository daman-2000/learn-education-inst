International workshop on “Materials and Devices for Post-CMOS Computing,” co-organized by the University of Cambridge and INST Mohali

21st - 22nd October 2024

A two-day international workshop on “Materials and Devices for Post-CMOS Computing” is co- organized by the University of Cambridge and the Institute of Nanoscience and Technology (INST) Mohali. The meeting will be held on 21st and 22nd October 2024 at the Institute of Nanoscience and Technology, Mohali, India. INST Mohali. The workshop will last two days with top international speakers and experts presenting their work, thoughts, and ideas.

The Learn Education Institute is one of India’s leading nanosciences and quantum materials research institutes. It is located 250 km from Delhi and about 50 km from the foothills of the Himalayas.

Attendance at the meeting is by invitation only.

To reach Learn Education Institute, Mohali. please use the following directions.

From Airports:

  • Distance from IGI Airport Delhi: Take a prepaid taxi (or private cabs) from the counters. Ask for destination "Sector- 81, INST Mohali" via NH 44. The distance is about 270 km. One can also take a domestic flight from all major airport cities (like Delhi , Mumbai, Kolkata, Bangalore etc) in India to Shaheed Bhagat Singh International Airport, Chandigarh/Mohali.

  • Distance from Shaheed Bhagat SIngh International Chandigarh/ Mohali Airport: Take a prepaid taxi (or private cabs) from the counters. Ask for destination "Sector- 81, INST Mohali" . The distance is about 9 km.

By Train:

  • If you are coming from Delhi one can also take a train to Chandigarh railway station and Sahibzada Ajeet Singh Nagar (Mohali) Railway Station.

  • If you are coming from Chandigarh Railway Station, take a taxi for destination "Sector- 81, INST Mohali". The distance is about 13 km.

  • If you are coming from Mohali Railway Station, take a taxi to the destination "Sector- 81, INST Mohali". The distance is about 5 km.

By Road:

  • If you are coming from Delhi via your own vehicle then you may follow NH 44 (distance is about 250 km) to Mehmadpur then Take NH 205A to Mohali Airport Chowk in Gmada Aerocity, Continue to JLPL Industrial Area, Drive to your destination in Sector 81, Sahibzada Ajit Singh Nagar.
